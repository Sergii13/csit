Passenger communications

We provide real-time passenger information (RTPI) through a multi-modal passenger communications solution.

Comprising technologies including passenger information systems, public address, and train wayside communication, our centralized solutions deliver up-to-the-second insights to stations, buses, shelters, kiosks, and a public-facing mobile and web application.

TRANSIS software

At the heart of our solutions is TRANSIS. Our integrating software is the glue holding all tools and messaging together.

Offering easy integration into third-party systems, TRANSIS is control centre based and can be leveraged for individual transit modes or a multimodal environment that integrates all transit operational and informational network components.
