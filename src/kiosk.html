Interactive
kiosk

Intelligent commuting

Interactive kiosks provide helpful in-station content pulled from multiple data sources.

These information hubs have an intuitive touchscreen interface, giving the public easy access to schedules, planners, emergency notifications, and engaging media.

Service status and messaging

Providing the public with real-time updates and information for various services.

  • Overall service status per service type and mode

  • Escalator and elevator status for reduced-mobility users

  • Service maps per mode with multi-touch functionality

  • Emergency messaging

  • Complementary media content

Navigation and accessibility

Make commuting easier and help your passengers plan their trip.

  • Real-time train/bus arrival or departure information

  • Trip planners

  • Station navigators

  • Neighbourhood maps

  • Complementary media content
